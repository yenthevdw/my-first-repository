Welcome to the cloud website,
where you'll be able to find a lot of different information about clouds. Let's get started.

Clouds can have all different kinds of sizes and shapes. On the left you can see one of the, I think, traditional clouds. If you would like to read more about clouds you can click on the about button above. There will be information about the types of clouds there are, the precipitation and the functions of clouds. Apart from reading all that information, I would also recommend to lay on the ground and stare into the sky, multiple times a day or a week. That way you can see different clouds yourself, make links between the weather and the clouds you see, and it is also very relaxing.
